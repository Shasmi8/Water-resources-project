Methods for conservation

  1. Under ground water - Aforestation:
    • Forest and other vegetation cover slow the surface runoff and replenish underground water.
  2. Water harvesting:
    • Water harvesting is another method to save surface runoff.
  3. Canal:
    • The canals used for irrigation fields should be properly lined to minimize losses by water seepage.
  4. Sprinkler system:
    • Sprinklers effectively irrigate the are by checking water losses through seepage and evaporation.
  5. Drip and trickle irrigation:
    • In dry regions with hight rates of evaporation, drip or trickle irrigation is very useful. The valuable water resource can therefore be conserved by adopting these means of irrigation .
