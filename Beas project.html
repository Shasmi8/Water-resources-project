Beas Project




Beas project is the biggest multi purpose project among India.This Rs 360 crore project which consists of tow units-Beas Sutlej Link and Beas Dam.Work for this project was started in 1962 initially at Pong village.The whole project was completed in June 1977.





Location and Uses
LocationUses
Pong village of Himachal Pradesh.
Beneficial states are Himachal Pradesh, Punjab, Haryana and Rajasthan
  1. provide irrigation facilities to 26 lakh square hectares of land.
  2. help in flood control.
  3. helps in developing of ferrying services for a stretch of 37 kms.
  4. generate 9 lakh kilowatt of electricity.
